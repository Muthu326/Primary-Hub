# Teacher's Access Kit: Primary Learn Hub 🏫📡

This guide helps you manage student access during the 100th-anniversary celebration.

## 1. Getting Students Online (The Blackboard Trick)
The easiest way for a whole class to join is via the **School WiFi**:
1.  Log in to the **Admin Panel** (`ADMIN100`).
2.  Go to the **"Access & URLs 📡"** tab.
3.  Look for the **Green Box**.
4.  Write that URL (e.g., `http://192.168.1.5:8501`) on the blackboard.
5.  Students type that into their tablets/phones.

## 2. Managing New Students
If a student doesn't have a token:
- They click **"Request New Access"** on the login page.
- You go to your Admin Panel -> **"Approve Requests 📝"** tab.
- Click **Approve** next to their name. They are now ready to learn!

## 3. Slow Internet? No Problem!
- The **WiFi Access URL** (Green Box) works even if the public internet is slow or down, as long as the WiFi router is on.
- It is much faster than the public website for a classroom full of students.

## 4. Monitoring Progress
- Use the **"Full Monitoring"** tab to see who is passing and which modules they are finishing.
- The **"Top 5 Rankings"** tab can be used to announce winners at the end of the day!
